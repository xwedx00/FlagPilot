### 1. Payment: Net 90 after "full stakeholder approval"

**Risk: MEDIUM** - Standard for custom work but timing matters
**Counter:**
- IP transfers upon full payment completion
- Retain rights to pre-existing tools, frameworks, and reusable components
- License for client use during development phase

### 3. Revisions: "Unlimited revisions until satisfaction"
**Risk: HIGH** - Scope creep nightmare
**Counter:**
- Include 3 rounds of revisions in base price
- Additional revisions: $150/hour with 5-business-day turnaround
- Define "satisfaction" as meeting agreed-upon specifications

### 4. Warranty: 2-year free maintenance and bug fixes
**Risk: HIGH** - Unsustainable for custom software
**Counter:**
- 90-day warranty covering critical bugs only
- Post-warranty: $125/hour with priority response
- Exclude issues from client modifications or third-party integrations

### 5. Liability: Developer liable for all business losses from software bugs
**Risk: HIGH** - Unlimited financial exposure
**Counter:**
- Limit liability to total contract value
- Exclude consequential damages (lost profits, data, reputation)
- Require client to maintain appropriate business insurance

### 6. Termination: Client can terminate without payment if "unsatisfied"
**Risk: HIGH** - No protection for work completed
**Counter:**
- Termination for convenience: pay for completed milestones + 20% of remaining contract
- Termination for cause: only for material breach with 15-day cure period
- Kill fee: 25% of remaining contract value for non-cause termination

### 7. Non-compete: Cannot work with competitors for 3 years
**Risk: MEDIUM** - Overly restrictive timeframe
**Counter:**
- 6-month non-compete limited to direct competitors in same industry
- Exclude general skills, methodologies, and publicly available information
- Geographic limitation to client's primary market area
